# Places

## 1. Brinewatch Harbor
- Type: Foggy coastal port
- Identity: Working fishing town turned informal trade hub; salt, nets, and shouted bargains
- Key features: Clattering wharves, tidal markets, lighthouse with a patched lens
- Culture & economy: Fisherfolk cooperatives, salvage divers, fermented-seafood cuisine
- Ambience: Cold wind, creaking wood, smell of brine and smoke

## 2. Emberfall Vale
- Type: Volcanic highland valley
- Identity: Resilient agrarian community built around geothermal terraces
- Key features: Hot-spring baths, obsidian artisans, terraced flame-gardens
- Culture & economy: Geothermal farming, glassmaking, ritual fire-dances
- Ambience: Warm steam, crackle of forges, orange dusk glow

## 3. Gilded Rift
- Type: Walled river city
- Identity: Wealthy mercantile capital with ornate canals and strict guilds
- Key features: Guild halls with gilded facades, floating bazaars, a central tax quay
- Culture & economy: Finance and craft guild oligarchy, elaborate public ceremonies
- Ambience: Polished stone, distant laughter, the clink of coin

## 4. Thistlefen
- Type: Misty peatland
- Identity: Scattered hamlets and hidden sanctuaries; strong folk magic tradition
- Key features: Boardwalk paths, reed-woven huts, standing stones with moss glyphs
- Culture & economy: Foragers, herbalists, peat-harvesters, oral lore-keepers
- Ambience: Soft footfalls on damp boards, insect hum, herbal smoke

## 5. Ironroot Hollow
- Type: Subterranean industrial hollow
- Identity: Engineered cavern-city anchored to iron veins and machine-cathedrals
- Key features: Conveyor bridges, piston workshops, a cathedral of gears
- Culture & economy: Miner-engineers, machinists, communal forges and ration halls
- Ambience: Rhythm of pistons, metallic tang, steady lamplight
